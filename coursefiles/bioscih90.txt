                             Biological Sciences

        REPEATS AND MAJOR RESTRICTIONS: Students who wish to enroll to
        repeat a course will be restricted from enrolling until 12 Noon
        on September 5th.

        PREREQUISITES: Students who have completed the equivalent of the
        prerequisites at another institution may become eligible to
        enroll in a particular UCI Bio Sci course by completing the form
        on the Bio Sci Student Affairs website:
        http://www.bio.uci.edu/students/undergraduates/course-pre-requisite-clearance-form/
        a minimum of two days before their window opens. Once students
        have had their prerequisites cleared they will remain eligible
        for one quarter, with the exception of Bio 199 (clearance
        remains for the academic year).

        LAB COURSES:

        BIO LAB ENROLLMENT POLICY!!!!

        THERE WILL BE NO DROPS OF ANY BIO SCI UPPER-DIVISION LABS and
        Bio Sci 100 AFTER September 27th AT 5PM.  This deadline is
        earlier than the Francisco J. Ayala School of Biological
        Sciences Week 2 deadline. 

        MINIMUM GRADE POLICY!!!!!

        Beginning Fall 2016, students must earn a grade of "D-" or above
        to advance to the next Bio core course. For example, you must
        earn a "D-" or better grade in Bio Sci 94 to move onto Bio Sci
        97, 98 and 99.
